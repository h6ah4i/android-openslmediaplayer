Privacy Policy

Last update: February 11, 2017

This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") applies to OpenSLMediaPlayer demo apps (both Java and C++ API versions), the Android apps developed by h6ah4i. The policy explains which information to be treated by the app and usages of them.

The apps do not collect nor send any personal identifiable data. In other words we never treat information such as your name, phone number, e-mail address, address or geographic location.

The apps capture audio from playing on the app. We use them only for the real-time audio visualization demo and never use them for any other purposes.
